# Language: Skill Doc + Note Output · 语言风格：Skill 文档 + 笔记输出

Two voices live here — the package's austere documentation voice and the note's
Chinese-first teaching voice. Both are self-contained.
本文件内嵌两种声音——包内冷峻文档体与笔记的中文教学体。二者均自洽。

---

## Voice A — Skill documentation | Voice A——Skill 文档

The package voice is fixed: zero filler; load-bearing verbs; em-dash
qualification; a short sentence to land the stress; honesty over rhetoric.
包内声音固定：零套话；承重动词；破折号用于限定；短句落重音；诚实压过修辞。

1. **Zero filler.** Cut “comprehensive / in-depth / very important”. Name the
   source, gate, artifact, or failure. | **零套话。** 删“全面／深入／十分重要”。直接写
   来源、闸门、工件或失败。
2. **Concrete verbs.** Use close, bind, extract, anchor, verify — not “process /
   handle / carry out”. | **具象动词。** 用闭合、绑定、抽取、锚定、核验——不用“处理／进行”。
3. **Qualification earns punctuation.** Use the em dash to narrow or reverse;
   use the colon for definition or consequence. | **标点须挣得。** 破折号用于收窄或反转；
   冒号用于定义或后果。
4. **State the break.** “Without the transcript, teacher insight cannot be
   attributed” is stronger than “read carefully”. | **写明断点。** “无转录稿则教师洞见无法
   归因”强于“请仔细阅读”。

---

## Voice B — Note output | Voice B——笔记输出

Write the note **Chinese first, terminologically exact, compact but sufficient**.
The prose teaches; the source discipline restrains it. Never mention a skill in
the deliverable.
笔记须**中文先行、术语精确、紧凑而充分**。语言负责教学；来源纪律负责克制。交付物中
不得点名任何 skill。

### Structure carries review | 用结构承载复习

1. **Open the prose with consequence.** After metadata and any ToC, use three to
   five sentences to state what the unit teaches, why the mechanism matters, and
   where it sits in the course. Do not open the prose with “this section mainly introduces”.
   **正文以后果开篇。** 元数据之后；若有目录，则在目录之后。用三至五句说明学什么、机制
   为何成立、位于课程何处。正文不用“本节主要介绍”开头。
2. **Define terms on first use.** Use `source-language term（中文）` once when
   translation is needed, then keep one stable form. Under the Stanford reference
   profile, the source term is normally English. Formulae and code retain their
   original notation.
   **术语首次即定义。** 需要翻译时，首次写 `源语言术语（中文）`，此后固定一种形式。
   Stanford 参照 profile 下源术语通常为英文。公式与代码保留原记法。
3. **Tables map; prose explains.** Use tables for term-definition or Part-role
   mappings. Follow each table with one sentence stating what the mapping changes.
   **表格映射，正文解释。** 术语—定义或 Part—角色用表格；表后一句说明映射改变了什么。
4. **Technical detail follows need.** Show only formulae, rules, or code fragments
   required to understand the mechanism; never paste a full lecture file.
   **技术细节服从需要。** 只展示理解机制所需的公式、规则或代码片段；不整段搬运讲义。

### Quotations carry instructor voice | 用引文承载教师声音

5. **Quote once, at the point of use.** Format each exact source-language sentence
   as an italic blockquote; when it is not Chinese, follow immediately with a
   Chinese translation. Under the Stanford reference profile, the source sentence
   is normally English. Do not repeat it in “Instructor Insights”.
   **一次引用，就地放置。** 精确源语言原句用斜体 blockquote；非中文时，下一行立即给中文
   翻译。Stanford 参照 profile 下原句通常为英文。不得在“教师洞见”再次重复。
6. **Comment after evidence.** Explain why the quotation changes the learner's
   model; do not praise it as “the most important insight”.
   **证据后再点评。** 说明引文如何改变学习者模型；不用“最重要的洞见”赞美。
7. **No stitched quotations.** Repeated ideas may be synthesized in editorial
   prose, never fused inside quotation marks.
   **不拼接引文。** 重复观点可在编辑性正文中综合，绝不熔进同一引号。

### Keep the course chain visible | 保持课程链可见

8. **End with position, not recap.** Show prior concept → current mechanism →
   next concept → assessment or practice. The close states what becomes possible next.
   **以定位收束，不复述。** 展示前置概念 → 当前机制 → 后续概念 → 考核或练习；结尾说明
   下一步因此可以做什么。
9. **Parent synthesis must stand alone.** Give a 3–5 sentence overview, the
   cross-unit chain, one connected distillation per child, representative
   quotations, earned cross-child insights, and the course/assessment path.
   Length follows aggregate coverage; a title list is not a synthesis.
   **父级综合必须独立可用。** 给三至五句概览、跨单元主线、各子单元的连贯浓缩、代表性
   引文、经证据赢得的跨子项洞见及课程/考核脉络。篇幅服从总覆盖量；标题清单不是综合。
10. **Metadata is factual and complete.** Enumerate every actual input in the
    live source field, record only verified relations, and use the real update
    date. Claim no completeness the audit did not earn.
    **元数据须事实完整。** 在真实来源字段列全实际输入，只记录经验证的关联，并使用真实
    更新日期。不声称未经审计赢得的“完整”。

## One-pass clean | 一键清洗流程

1. Delete oral filler, logistics, repeated transitions, and mood-adjectives. |
   删口语填充、课程后勤、重复过渡与情绪形容词。
2. Check each concept has a mechanism or teacher contribution, not merely a label. |
   检查每个概念都有机制或教师增量，而非仅有标签。
3. Search every quotation; remove or repair any non-verbatim line. |
   搜索每条引文；删除或修正非逐字内容。
4. Replace unexplained jargon with one precise first-use definition. |
   给未解释术语补一次精准定义。
5. Remove duplicated quotations and duplicated section conclusions. |
   删除重复引文与重复章节结论。
6. Read the final path alone: it must reveal the course-to-assessment chain. |
   单读末尾脉络：它必须显出课程—考核链。
7. Reconcile the metadata against the input ledger; no source may disappear. |
   用输入台账反查元数据；任何来源不得消失。
