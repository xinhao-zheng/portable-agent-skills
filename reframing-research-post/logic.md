# Reframing Research Post Skeleton · 重定义式研究帖推进骨架

Use this domain-neutral sequence when the value must come from a better
explanation, not a longer summary. Treat it as a backstage inference protocol,
not as mandatory published headings. The reframe is the result. Never make it the
premise.
当稿件价值须来自更好的解释、而非更长的摘要时，使用这套领域中性顺序。把它当作幕后的
推断协议，而非必须照搬的成稿标题。重定义是结果。绝不能是前提。

---

## Order of advance | 推进顺序（九步推断闸门）

### 1. Bound the corpus and type every claim | 界定语料，给主张分型

State the decision question, audience, unit of analysis, horizon and cutoff, and
material set. Build a source manifest with date, primary/secondary status, method,
scope, and known interest. Documents that repeat one upstream source count as one
origin, not several confirmations.
先定决策问题、受众、分析单位、时间窗与截止日、材料集合。来源清单须含日期、一手/二手、
方法、范围与已知利益关系。复述同一上游的多份材料，只算一个证据源。

Use this fixed claim-state set backstage:
幕后只用以下固定主张状态：

| State · 状态 | Gate · 闸门 |
|---|---|
| `observed` · 观察 | Exact source address · 精确来源地址 |
| `calculated` · 计算 | Sourced inputs + visible arithmetic · 输入有出处＋算式可见 |
| `estimated` · 估计 | Model + assumptions + sensitivity · 模型＋假设＋敏感性 |
| `inferred` · 推断 | Bridge + rival + falsifier · 桥梁＋竞争解释＋证伪 |
| `scenario` · 情景 | Trigger + horizon + update rule · 触发＋时间窗＋更新规则 |
| `unknown` · 未知 | Preserve the gap; do not fill it · 保留缺口，不补造 |

Do not write the thesis yet. Premature compression turns research into a search
for supporting quotes.
此时不要写论题。过早压缩会把研究变成寻找支持性引文。

### 2. Map the system before naming the thesis | 先画系统，后命名论题

Map actors and flows: money, material, capacity, information, attention, trust,
risk, and time. Ask what each actor receives, pays, controls, waits for, and can
pass downstream. Name the different engineering, regulatory, adoption, financing,
and accounting clocks.
画出行动者与资源流：钱、物料、产能、信息、注意力、信任、风险与时间。逐个追问得到什么、
支付什么、控制什么、等待什么、又能向下游转嫁什么；同时点名工程、监管、采用、融资与
会计确认的不同节奏。

For a multi-business system, trace operating cash flow → internal transfer →
recipient project → return clock. Separate the funding asset's standalone quality,
its no-transfer counterfactual, and the recipient project's marginal return.
多业务系统须追踪“经营现金流 → 内部转移 → 受资项目 → 回报时钟”，并分开供血资产的
独立质量、不转移时的反事实，以及受资项目的边际回报。

A constraint is binding only if relaxing it changes the outcome. Test who bears
it, when it bites, whether it can be passed on or substituted, whether it blocks
demonstration or replication, and what observable moves when it loosens.
只有“放松后会改变结果”的约束才算绑定。检验谁承担、何时发生、能否转嫁或替代、卡住
演示还是复制，以及约束放松时哪个可观察项应变化。

### 3. Let frames compete; earn the reframe | 让框架竞争，赢得重定义

Steelman the consensus: state what it says, why informed people believe it, and
which facts it explains. Then compare it with at least one material rival and,
when distinct, a null frame on evidence coverage, contradictions, unsupported
assumptions, distinctive predictions, and decision consequence.
先把共识强化到最强：写清它说什么、知情者为何相信、能解释哪些事实。再让它与至少一个
实质竞争框架，以及可区分时的零假设比较：证据覆盖、矛盾、无证假设、区分性预测与决策后果。

Keep the frame that survives. Allow the consensus to win; if no frame is
identified, preserve the competition as an open question. Only then compress:
保留经竞争后站住的框架。允许共识胜出；若无法识别胜者，把竞争保留为开放问题。此时才
压缩论题：

`X is usually read as A; B better explains the evidence because C; the claim weakens or fails if D.`
`X 通常被看作 A；B 因 C 对现有证据解释力更强；若 D 出现，本主张减弱或失效。`

### 4. Triangulate independent evidence | 三角验证独立证据

Align entity, definition, unit, currency, denominator, sample, date, and horizon
before comparing. Stock vs flow, total vs tradable, average vs marginal, or nominal
vs real fails the gate.
比较前先对齐实体、定义、单位、币种、分母、样本、日期与时间窗。存量混流量、总量混
可交易量、平均混边际、名义混实际者，判失败。

Triangulate load-bearing claims across independent origins and, where possible,
across system layers or horizons. Surface conflicts; explain whether they come
from method, date, definition, incentive, or genuine uncertainty. Never settle
them by majority vote.
承重主张须跨独立上游验证；条件允许时，再跨系统层级或时间窗。暴露冲突，并判断差异来自
方法、日期、定义、利益关系，还是确有未知。不得以多数票消解冲突。

For cross-industry read-through, name the transaction, quantity exposure, price
pass-through, timing, and substitute path. Supplier orders may prove the buyer's
bill; they do not by themselves prove the buyer's value.
跨产业映射须点明交易关系、数量暴露、价格传导、发生时点与替代路径。供应商订单可以证明
买方账单；不能单独证明买方价值。

Keep source + date + page/section/link beside each load-bearing fact. For every
derived number show: sourced inputs → formula → result → meaning → largest
sensitivity. Arithmetic never substitutes for sourced inputs.
每个承重事实近处标来源＋日期＋页码/章节/链接。每个推导数摊开：带出处的输入 → 公式 →
结果 → 含义 → 最大敏感项。算式绝不替代输入出处。

### 5. Pass every causal edge through a test | 逐条检验因果边

For every `A → B`, test seven things: temporal order; transmission carrier;
evidence for the edge rather than A and B separately; rival or confounder;
counterfactual prediction; direction, magnitude, and lag; boundary or reversal.
每条 `A → B` 均检验七项：时间顺序；传导载体；支撑“边”而非只分别支撑 A、B 的证据；
替代解释或混杂；反事实预测；方向、量级与滞后；边界或反转条件。

If an edge fails, call it an association or working hypothesis. Do not let an
arrow turn correlation into cause.
因果边过不了闸门，就只称相关或工作假设。不得让箭头把相关变成因果。

Bridge every decision or valuation claim from mechanism to output. At minimum,
show sign and order of magnitude; where data permit, model timed benefits and
costs rather than clustering external targets.
每个决策或估值主张都须由机制桥接到输出量。最低要求是方向与数量级；数据允许时，直接
估计分时收益与成本，不聚类外部目标数字。

### 6. Find constraint transfers and hidden bills | 寻找约束转移与隐藏账单

Translate every attractive claim into a timed transaction: who receives the
benefit; who pays cash, time, capacity, trust, or optionality; who can pass the
bill on; and what changes at scale.
把每条诱人主张翻译成带时间的交易：谁得益；谁支付现金、时间、产能、信任或选择权；谁能
转嫁；规模化后什么会变。

Ask whether an advantage removes a constraint or internalizes it. Separate
demonstration from replication, technical success from unit economics, and gross
benefit from net value. Before something becomes a moat, name what must be paid,
learned, or believed first.
追问优势究竟消灭约束，还是把它内部化。分开演示与复制、技术成功与单位经济、毛收益与
净价值。在某物成为护城河前，点明必须先支付、学会或被相信什么。

Test whether the same strategy changes sign across system layers: control of a
scarce upstream input may add value, while control of a neutral interface may
damage complementors, customer trust, or multi-homing. Never export one layer's
integration verdict to another unchanged.
检验同一策略是否跨层变号：控制稀缺上游可能增值，控制中立接口却可能损害互补生态、客户
信任或多归属。不得把一个层级的整合结论原样外推到另一层。

Audit optimism and pessimism symmetrically: find the hidden bill inside an upside
story and the hidden option inside a downside story. Fixed contrarianism is just
another consensus.
对乐观与悲观叙事做对称审计：既找上行故事里的隐藏账单，也找下行故事里的隐藏期权。固定
逆向，不过是另一种共识。

### 7. Derive cross-insights and map feedback | 推出交叉洞见，画出反馈

A cross-insight passes only if no single source states it, at least two independent
chains support it, an explicit mechanism joins them, and a rival or falsifier can
separate insight from story. Use:
交叉洞见须满足：没有单一来源直接说出；至少两条独立链支撑；两链间有明确机制；有竞争
解释或证伪把洞见与故事分开。使用：

`Premise A + Premise B → bridge → new conclusion → rival → discriminating observation.`
`前提 A＋前提 B → 桥梁 → 新结论 → 竞争解释 → 区分性观察。`

Run the one-source deletion test: remove either chain; the conclusion must weaken.
Map feedback only after naming the initial shock, then identify the reinforcing
loop, balancing loop, slow variable, and loop-breaker.
跑“删除一源”测试：抽掉任一链，结论必须减弱。只有先点名初始冲击，才画反馈；随后识别
强化回路、制衡回路、慢变量与断环条件。

Discover the failure mode; do not name it in advance. Cross-check local result vs
system objective, pilot vs scale, benefit clock vs cost/funding clock, KPI vs real
outcome, one actor's incentive vs system outcome, first-order gain vs second-order
feedback, and standalone quality vs internal allocation.
发现失败形态，而非预先命名。交叉检查：局部结果 vs 系统目标、试点 vs 规模、收益时钟 vs
成本/融资时钟、KPI vs 真实结果、单一行动者激励 vs 系统结果、一阶收益 vs 二阶反馈、
独立质量 vs 内部配置。

Generate candidates, then retain only those supported by evidence and a
discriminating prediction. Company research may reveal scale, timing,
monetization, financing, integration, allocation, metric, coordination, lock-in,
success-reversal, or half-success traps; other fields translate the same relations.
“No identified trap” is valid. Novelty is not a quota.
先生成候选，再只保留有证据与区分性预测者。公司研究可能识别规模、时间、变现、融资、整合、
配置、指标、协同、锁定、成功反噬或半成功等陷阱；其他领域迁移同一关系。“未识别陷阱”
也是有效结论。新奇不是配额。

### 8. Land in a calibrated conditional judgment | 落到校准后的条件判断

Separate present proof from future option, quality from price, demand from
monetization, progress from replication, and possibility from odds. Use the fixed
confidence set `established / supported / tentative / unknown`; never invent a
probability because the format asks for one.
分开当前证据与未来期权、质量与价格、需求与变现、进展与复制、可能性与概率。只用固定
置信词面 `established / supported / tentative / unknown`；不得因格式需要编概率。

Choose scenarios, ranges, time windows, tracking variables, thresholds, or an
evidence gap to match the decision. Each case names assumptions, horizon, leading
indicators, largest sensitivity, falsifier, and update rule. A range without a
bridge is no more rigorous than an adjective.
按决策选择情景、区间、时间窗、跟踪变量、阈值或证据缺口。每种情形点明假设、时间窗、
领先指标、最大敏感项、证伪与更新规则。没有桥梁的区间，并不比形容词严谨。

For valuation or allocation, derive net value from the mechanism: timed realized
benefit + avoided external cost + option value − capex − operating and learning
cost − financing and dilution − execution risk. Adapt the terms to the domain;
never average unlike targets into a “neutral” answer.
估值或配置须由机制推净值：分时兑现收益＋避免的外部成本＋期权价值－资本开支－运营与
学习成本－融资与稀释－执行风险。按领域替换项；绝不把不同方法的目标平均成“中性”答案。

### 9. Compose an irreversible argument | 写成不可调换的论证

Make the title state the earned thesis. If the consensus wins, state the calibrated
confirmation; if no frame wins, state the open question. Use a reversal only when
evidence changes the old frame. In the first screen state the old frame, result,
binding contradiction, and why now.
标题须陈述经证据赢得的论题。共识胜出时写校准后的确认；无法识别时写开放问题；只有证据
改变旧框架时才使用反转。第一屏写清旧框架、结果、绑定矛盾与为何是现在。

`plausible consensus → system → earned thesis → cross-evidence → hidden bill → feedback / identified failure mode → conditional judgment → strongest rival → coda`
`可信共识 → 系统 → 赢得的论题 → 交叉证据 → 隐藏账单 → 反馈／已识别失败形态 → 条件判断 → 最强竞争解释 → 收束`

Each section inherits a conclusion and creates the next question. If adjacent
sections can swap without damage, the piece is still a pile. Meet the strongest
rival model; close by returning to the opening contradiction, with no new evidence.
每节承接一个结论并逼出下一问。若相邻两节可无损互换，全文仍是材料堆。正面处理最强竞争
模型；回到开篇矛盾收束，不添新证据。

## Stop conditions | 停止条件

- Preserve the source frame when the user asks for faithful summary. |
  用户要忠实摘要时，保留源框架。
- When material is thin, stale, or conflicting, return hypotheses and evidence
  gaps, not a causal verdict. | 材料过少、过期或冲突时，返回假设与证据缺口，不交付因果裁决。
- State association when causality is not identified; verify current or high-stakes
  claims against primary or authoritative sources. | 因果无法识别时只写相关；时效性或高风险
  主张须对照当前一手或权威来源。
- Never invent data, quotations, citations, inputs, or a reframe the evidence did
  not earn. | 绝不补造数据、引文、引用、输入或证据未赢得的重定义。

## Self-check | 自检清单

- [ ] Are corpus, cutoff, source independence, and claim states explicit? |
      语料、截止日、来源独立性与主张状态是否明确？
- [ ] Could the consensus genuinely have won the frame competition? |
      共识是否真有可能在框架竞争中胜出？
- [ ] Do facts have exact sources and derived numbers have inputs, arithmetic, and
      sensitivity? | 事实是否有精确来源；推导数是否有输入、算式与敏感性？
- [ ] Does each causal edge pass time, mechanism, edge evidence, rival,
      counterfactual, magnitude/lag, and boundary? | 每条因果边是否通过时间、机制、
      边上证据、竞争解释、反事实、量级/滞后与边界？
- [ ] Is the constraint binding; are incidence, pass-through, substitution, and
      scale named? | 约束是否绑定；承担、转嫁、替代与规模是否点明？
- [ ] Does each cross-insight join two independent chains and weaken when one is
      removed? | 每条交叉洞见是否连接两条独立链，并在删除一源后减弱？
- [ ] Are optimism, pessimism, feedback, and candidate failure modes audited
      symmetrically — including “no trap”? | 乐观、悲观、反馈与候选失败形态是否被对称
      审计——包括“未识别陷阱”？
- [ ] Does the final judgment follow from mechanism and name confidence,
      sensitivity, falsifier, and update rule? | 最终判断是否由机制推出，并点明置信、
      敏感项、证伪与更新规则？
- [ ] Would swapping sections break the argument, and does the coda add no evidence? |
      调换章节是否会破坏论证；收束是否不添证据？
