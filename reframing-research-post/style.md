# Language: Skill Doc + Research-Post Output · 语言风格：Skill 文档 + 研究帖输出

Use two voices, separated by artifact type. Both are self-contained; neither
requires a sibling skill.
按工件类型使用两种声音。二者均自洽，不依赖 sibling skill。

---

## Voice A — Skill documentation | Voice A——Skill 文档

Write `SKILL.md`, `logic.md`, and this file in the package's austere analytical
voice: one narrow claim, causal advance, load-bearing words, explicit limits,
and contrast only after proof.
`SKILL.md`、`logic.md` 与本文件统一使用包内冷峻思辨文档体：一条收窄主张、因果推进、
每词承重、边界明示，证明之后才允许对照。

1. **Zero filler.** Cut “comprehensive / in-depth / worth noting”. Name the step,
   gate, failure, or output. | **零套话。** 删“全面／深入／值得注意”。直接写步骤、闸门、
   失败或输出。
2. **Concrete verbs.** Use bind, transfer, trace, test, derive, weaken, falsify —
   not “conduct / carry out / make clearer”. | **具象动词。** 用绑定、转移、追溯、检验、
   推出、削弱、证伪——不用“进行／开展／使其更清晰”。
3. **Qualification earns the em dash.** Use it to narrow or reverse a claim, not
   as decoration. | **破折号承载限定。** 只用于收窄或反转主张，不作装饰。
4. **Honesty over force.** “The evidence does not identify this” is stronger than
   unsupported certainty. | **诚实压过强硬。** “现有证据无法识别”强于无证确定性。

## Voice B — Research-post output | Voice B——研究帖文体

Write the post **austere in inference, plain in language, progressive in
discovery**. A non-specialist should follow the mechanism; a specialist should
locate the source, reproduce the calculation, and see where inference begins.
Never name a skill in the deliverable.
研究帖须**推断冷峻、语言白话、发现递进**。外行能跟上机制；内行能定位来源、复算数字、
看出推断从哪里开始。交付物中绝不点名任何 skill。

The target lies between three failures:
目标位于三种失败之间：

- ✗ **report dump · 报告堆砌** — facts and charts, no derived thesis · 有事实有图表，无推出的论题；
- ✗ **hot take · 热点断言** — conclusion first, no audit trail · 先有结论，无审计链；
- ✗ **AI insight tone · AI 洞见腔** — repeated reversals without new evidence · 反复反转，无新增证据；
- ✓ **right · 对** — plain claim → exact evidence → bridge → rival → conditional judgment ·
  白话主张 → 精确证据 → 桥梁 → 竞争解释 → 条件判断。

### Lead with meaning | 意思先行

1. **Land the point in one human sentence, then show why.** Keep jargon,
   citations, and source inventories behind the meaning. Make one paragraph do
   one inferential job.
   **先用一句人话落点，再说明为什么。** 术语、引用与来源清单放在意思之后；一段只做
   一项推断工作。

   - ✓ *“Demand remains; delivery is blocked by approvals and equipment.”* ·
     “需求没有消失；交付卡在审批和设备。”
   - ✗ *“This article presents a comprehensive multi-dimensional analysis.”* ·
     “本文将从多个维度展开综合分析。”

### Make status audible | 让证据状态听得出来

2. **Match verbs to status.** “The filing states” marks observation;
   “subtracting A from B gives” marks calculation; “taken together, these suggest”
   marks inference; “if X, then” marks scenario. Never call an inference a fact.
   **让动词匹配状态。** “公告显示”标观察；“B 减 A 得”标计算；“两项合看可推”标推断；
   “若 X，则”标情景。不得把推断叫事实。

3. **Calibrate strong words.** “Only / proves / real / rational / pure sentiment”
   require eliminated rivals. Otherwise use “better explains / is consistent
   with / more likely / tentative”.
   **校准强词。** “唯一／证明／真正／理性／纯情绪”须以排除竞争解释为前提；否则写
   “解释力更强／与……一致／更可能／暂定”。

### Keep plain language load-bearing | 白话，但每词承重

4. **Explain each term once.** Give the plain meaning first, then the technical
   term in parentheses. Use named subjects and concrete verbs: who pays, delays,
   approves, reprices, or absorbs risk.
   **术语只拆一次。** 先讲人话，再把术语放括号。使用具名主语和具象动词：谁付款、拖延、
   审批、重定价或吸收风险。

5. **Keep dialogue measured.** Ask the reader's real question at a turn, then
   answer it. Do not simulate intimacy with “obviously / scared yet / you know”.
   **对话感须克制。** 转折处替读者问出真实问题，再回答；不用“显然／怕了吗／你懂的”
   模拟亲近。

### Let syntax carry the argument | 让句法承载论证

6. **Use cause words, not ordinal scaffolding.** Prefer “because / therefore /
   otherwise / until” when the relationship is causal. Use the em dash for a
   paid-for reversal and the colon for definition or consequence.
   **用因果词，不用序号撑架。** 关系是因果时，用“因为／所以／否则／直到”。破折号承载
   有证据的反转，冒号承担定义或后果。

7. **Vary pressure.** Let one longer sentence carry the mechanism, then a short
   sentence land the consequence. Do not make every sentence a slogan.
   **长短句调压。** 长句承载机制，短句落后果。不得句句金句。

### Make titles and sections advance | 让标题与章节推进

8. **Make the title state the earned thesis.** Use a calibrated confirmation when
   consensus wins, an open question when evidence cannot decide, and a reversal
   only when evidence changes the frame. Keep headings short, specific, and free
   of emoji or engineering code.
   **标题陈述经证据赢得的论题。** 共识胜出时写校准确认，无法识别时写开放问题，只有证据
   改变框架时才反转。小节标题简短、具体，不用 emoji 或工程代号。

9. **Do not repeat the controlling phrase as progress.** Keep it for the opening
   and coda; between them, every return adds evidence, mechanism, or consequence.
   **不得用重复主句冒充推进。** 核心对照留给开篇与结尾；中间每次回返都须新增证据、机制
   或后果。

### Keep evidence close | 让证据贴近主张

10. **Cite specifically in plain words.** Put source, date, and page/section/link
    beside the fact. Put each formula beside its conclusion; show inputs, result,
    meaning, and sensitivity. An unexplained residual is not automatically a
    “story premium”.
    **用人话做精确近端引用。** 在事实旁点名来源、日期与页码／章节／链接；在结论旁放公式，
    写清输入、结果、含义与敏感性。未解释残差不自动等于“故事溢价”。

### Use form only where needed | 关系需要时才用形式

11. **Use tables for mappings, contradictions, scenarios, sensitivities, or
    tracking variables.** Follow each with one plain takeaway. Declare any graded
    lexical set once; use no emoji, colors, or improvised row labels.
    **表格只用于映射、矛盾、情景、敏感性或跟踪变量。** 表后接一句白话落点。定级词面
    只声明一次；不用 emoji、颜色或逐行即兴标签。

12. **Use figures as evidence, not decoration.** Explain how to read the relevant
    feature and why it changes the next inference. Remove production placeholders
    from publish-ready output unless the user requests an editing plan.
    **图是证据，不是装饰。** 讲清相关部位怎么看、为何改变下一步推断。发布态删制作占位；
    用户明确要编辑计划时除外。

### Mark rivals and limits plainly | 直说竞争解释与边界

13. **State the strongest rival model in its strongest form.** Name the
    observation that distinguishes it. Then state what remains unidentified,
    which estimate drives the result, and what evidence would change the view.
    **以最强形式陈述最强竞争模型。** 点明区分它的观察；再写尚未识别什么、哪个估计最影响
    结果、什么证据会改变判断。

### Emphasize and close with restraint | 克制强调，克制收束

14. **Bold only load-bearing lines.** Reserve bold for the earned thesis, decisive
    mechanism, conditional judgment, or coda. Close on the thesis or contrast that
    returns to the opening; add no new evidence.
    **只加粗承重句。** 加粗留给赢得的论题、决定性机制、条件判断或收束。以回响开篇的论题
    或对照结束；不添新证据。

15. **Let length follow inferential depth, not source count.** Preserve the
    user's accurate, distinctive wording; cut unsupported certainty, repetition,
    filler, and production notes. Add *非投资建议* for market subjects.
    **篇幅随推断深度，不随来源数。** 保留用户准确、独特的措辞；删无证确定性、重复、套话
    与制作备注。涉及市场时附*非投资建议*。

## One-pass clean | 一键清洗流程（成稿前）

1. Delete filler, mood-adjectives, clickbait, and simulated intimacy. |
   删套话、情绪形容词、标题党与模拟亲近。
2. Replace passive abstractions with a named actor and concrete verb. |
   把被动抽象句换成具名行动者与具象动词。
3. Match status verbs; explain each first-use term once. |
   校准状态动词；术语首次出现只拆一次。
4. Move sources to facts and formulas to conclusions. |
   把来源移到事实近处，把公式移到结论近处。
5. Keep only tables and figures that change the inference; add a plain takeaway. |
   只留会改变推断的表图，并补一句白话落点。
6. Break slogan chains; cut intermediate repeats of the controlling phrase. |
   拆掉金句串；删除核心主句在中段的无效重复。
7. Remove placeholders, broken links, emoji, heading codes, and raw source dumps. |
   删制作占位、坏链、emoji、标题代号与原始来源堆。
8. Read the last paragraph alone: it must state the conditional judgment and
   earned contrast, with no new evidence. | 单读末段：须给条件判断与赢得的对照，且无新证据。
