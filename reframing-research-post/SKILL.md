# Reframing Research Post · 重定义式研究帖

## What this is | 这是什么

In one line: **a method for finding the explanation that survives the evidence —
bound the material, let rival frames compete, derive the non-obvious claim from
independent evidence chains, and write the result in language a non-specialist can
follow and a specialist can audit.**
一句话：**一种找出“经证据竞争后仍站得住的解释”的方法——先界定材料，让不同框架竞争，
再从独立证据链的交叉处推出非显然主张，最后写成外行跟得上、内行查得动的研究帖。**

Its register is **austere in inference, plain in delivery**: narrow the claim,
show the causal bridge, expose what remains unknown, and spend rhetoric only where
the evidence bears it. The output may be conversational; the reasoning may not be
casual.
它的调性是**推断冷峻、表达白话**：收窄主张，摊开因果桥梁，点明仍未知之处，只在证据
承得住时使用修辞。成稿可以有对话感；推理不能随意。

Its opposite is two failures. The first is a **material dump**: sources retold in
order, with no thesis the sources did not already state. The second is a
**performative reframe**: “X is not A but B” written first, then decorated with
facts. One has evidence without inference; the other has novelty without proof.
它的反面有两种。第一种是**材料堆砌**：按来源顺序复述，全文没有一条超出原文的主张。
第二种是**表演式重定义**：先写“X 不是 A，而是 B”，再拿事实装饰。前者有证据无推断；
后者有新奇无证明。

## Style fingerprint | 风格指纹（九项，与 logic.md 九步一一对应）

1. **Bound corpus, typed claims** — state the question, horizon, source set, and
   cutoff; separate observation, calculation, estimate, inference, scenario, and
   unknown before composing.
   **界定语料、主张分型**——先定问题、时间窗、来源集合与截止日；落笔前区分观察、计算、
   估计、推断、情景与未知。
2. **System before thesis** — map actors, flows, incentives, clocks, and the
   binding constraint before deciding what the object “really is”.
   **先画系统、后定论题**——先画清行动者、资源流、激励、时钟与真正绑定的约束，再判断
   对象“究竟是什么”。
3. **Earned reframe** — let the consensus, at least one material rival, and a
   distinct null frame when applicable compete; keep the frame that explains more
   with fewer unsupported assumptions.
   **重定义须赢得**——让共识框架、至少一个实质竞争框架，以及适用时可区分的零假设同场
   竞争；保留解释更多、无证假设更少者。
4. **Independent triangulation** — align definitions and dates, trace shared
   upstream sources, surface contradictions, and attach source + date + page or
   section to every load-bearing fact; a derived number needs sourced inputs,
   visible arithmetic, and sensitivity.
   **独立三角验证**——统一定义与日期、追溯共同上游、暴露冲突；每个承重事实贴近标注
   来源＋日期＋页码或章节；推导数字同时要有输入出处、可见算式与敏感性。
5. **Causal edge gate** — for every A → B, name the mechanism, temporal order,
   evidence, rival explanation, counterfactual prediction, lag, and boundary;
   otherwise call it an association or hypothesis.
   **因果边闸门**——每条 A → B 都点明机制、时间顺序、证据、替代解释、反事实预测、
   滞后与边界；否则只称相关或假设。
6. **Constraint transfer, hidden bill** — ask whether an advantage removes a
   constraint or merely moves its cost, timing, risk, or bargaining power; name
   who pays first, who can pass it on, and what changes at scale.
   **约束转移、隐藏账单**——追问优势究竟消灭约束，还是只搬走成本、时间、风险或议价权；
   点明谁先付、能否转嫁、规模化后什么会变。
7. **Cross-insight + failure-mode discovery** — derive each load-bearing
   cross-insight from independent evidence chains; keep a one-source idea tentative;
   map feedback, generate candidate failure modes, and retain only what evidence
   distinguishes — including none. Half-success is one possible pattern, never the
   default answer.
   **交叉洞见＋失败形态发现**——承重交叉洞见由独立证据链推出；单源想法保持暂定；再画
   反馈、生成候选失败形态，只保留证据能区分者——也可以一个都没有。半成功只是一种可能，
   绝非默认答案。
8. **Calibrated judgment** — separate present proof from future option and quality
   from price; use scenarios, ranges, windows, or decision rules only when their
   assumptions, leading indicators, sensitivities, update rules, and falsifiers
   are named.
   **校准判断**——分开当前证据与未来期权、质量与价格；只有在假设、领先指标、敏感项、
   更新规则与证伪条件均点明时，才使用情景、区间、时间窗或决策规则。
9. **Irreversible composition** — make each section answer the prior question and
   force the next; steelman the consensus, meet the strongest rival, and close on
   the thesis or contrast the evidence has earned.
   **不可调换的推进**——每节回答上节问题并逼出下一问；公平强化共识，正面处理最强竞争
   解释，最后收在由证据赢得的论题或对照上。

## How to use | 怎么用

**Writing from material** — read [logic.md](logic.md) first and complete the
backstage evidence and inference gates (corpus → system → competing frames →
triangulation → causal tests → hidden bill → cross-insight → judgment →
composition). Then write per [style.md](style.md): skill documentation in the
austere voice (Voice A); the post in the plain research voice (Voice B).
**从材料写新稿**——先读 [logic.md](logic.md)，在幕后跑完证据与推断闸门（语料 → 系统 →
框架竞争 → 三角验证 → 因果检验 → 隐藏账单 → 交叉洞见 → 判断 → 成文）；再依
[style.md](style.md) 落笔：skill 文档用冷峻体（Voice A），研究帖用白话研究体（Voice B）。

**Reframing an existing draft** — audit the thesis before polishing the prose.
Split facts from inferences, reconstruct rival explanations, repair unsupported
causal edges, and quantify the bridge from mechanism to decision. Only then clean
the language. Do not preserve a fluent claim that the evidence cannot carry.
**重构已有草稿**——先审论题，后润语言。拆开事实与推断，重建竞争解释，修复无证因果边，
把机制到决策的量化桥梁补齐，再清洗句子。证据承不住的流畅主张，不予保留。

**When the material does not identify the answer** — stop at a hypothesis map,
evidence gap, or research plan. Label the unknown; do not manufacture a reframe,
number, quotation, source, or certainty.
**材料无法识别答案时**——止于假设图、证据缺口或研究计划。把未知写成未知；不补造
重定义、数字、引文、来源或确定性。

## One contrast | 一个最小对照

> Before (material dump) — *“Electric buses use less energy and need less
> maintenance. The city should electrify faster.”*
> 原写法（材料堆砌）——“电动公交能耗更低、维护更少。城市应该更快电动化。”

> Before (performative reframe) — *“Electrification is not a saving; it is a grid
> trap.”* No rival, no system boundary, no test.
> 原写法（表演式重定义）——“电动化不是省钱，而是电网陷阱。”无竞争解释、无系统边界、
> 无检验。

> After (this skill) — *“Lower energy and mature maintenance costs prove a saving
> at vehicle level, not yet at fleet level. If depot capacity leaves buses idle,
> capital cost spreads across fewer service kilometres. Electrification becomes a
> cost-saving program only when capacity raises utilization without moving the bill
> into financing and backup fleet. Until then, local savings are observed; system
> savings remain unproved.”*
> 改后（本 skill）——“能耗与成熟期维护成本下降，证明的是单车降本，还不是车队净节省。
> 若场站容量让车辆闲置，资本成本就会摊到更少的服务公里上。只有扩容提高利用率、且没有把
> 账单搬进融资和备车，电动化才成为省钱项目。在那之前，局部节省已观察；系统节省未证明。”

The difference is entirely: the conclusion is new, but every step that makes it
new is visible — source, bridge, rival, timing, and the observation that would
break it.
差别全在：结论是新的，但使它成立的每一步都看得见——来源、桥梁、竞争解释、时间顺序，
以及会推翻它的观察。
