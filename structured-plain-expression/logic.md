# Document Skeleton · 研究帖推进骨架

The advance order of a structured plain-language research post. The illustrations
draw on one quant case for concreteness; the skeleton is field-independent — it
holds for any data-backed study: an experiment report, a benchmark review, a
market post-mortem.
本文件给出结构化白话研究帖的推进顺序。范例取自同一个量化案例以求具体；骨架与领域
无关——任何有数据支撑的研究皆适用：实验报告、基准测评、行情复盘。

---

## Order of advance | 推进顺序（默认骨架）

1. **Title is the finding** — the title states one falsifiable conclusion, not a
   topic label; a one-line front matter follows (what this post studies — plus
   *非投资建议* when the subject is a market).
   **标题即结论**——标题陈述一条可证伪的结论，而非题材标签；紧跟一行 front matter
   （这篇在研究什么——涉市场则外加*非投资建议*）。
   - e.g. *"中长期概念 IC 平时无效，它的突然加速是抱团行情的警报"* ·
     *"新索引没有加速查询：P99 的改善全部来自缓存命中率上升"*
   - Anti-pattern (a topic label, not a finding): *"关于概念动量 IC 的一些观察"*。
     反模式（题材标签，非结论）。

2. **Overview first: a 30-second lookup** — within the first 200 words, one table
   (reader's question / our answer / which figure) closed by a one-line thesis; a
   reader who stops at the first screen still leaves with 80%.
   **概览先行：30 秒速查**——开头 200 字内一张表（读者会问／我们的答案／看哪张图），
   一句话主张收束；读者只读第一屏也带走八成。

3. **Problem first, then method** — section 1 opens on the *anomaly you found*
   (with a small before/after table of numbers), then *why this metric/method* —
   including the deeper reason it sees what ordinary tools cannot — then a route
   table of *how you'll check it*.
   **先讲问题，再讲方法**——第 1 节先摆"发现的反常现象"（配一张前后对照的数字小表），
   再讲"为什么用这个指标／方法"——含它比常规手段多看到什么的深层原因——最后给一张
   "怎么排查"的路线表。
   - Show the metric's edge in plain words: e.g. a ranking IC reads the whole
     cross-section's *relative structure* (a high-dimensional snapshot), which a
     single-asset price/volume chart cannot. · 用白话点出指标的独特处：如排名 IC 读的是
     整个横截面的*相对结构*（一张高维快照），单标的量价照不到。

4. **Evidence as a chain, figure by figure** — section 2 groups 2–3 figures per
   sub-section; each figure runs the four beats: how to read it → what it shows →
   how it links to the next → one bolded takeaway. Test: if two figures swap order
   with nothing breaking, the chain isn't progressive yet.
   **证据成链，逐图推进**——第 2 节每小节 2–3 张图；每张走四拍：怎么看 → 看出什么 →
   和下一张怎么接 → 一句加粗收束。检验：若两图调换顺序也不影响，说明还没成链。

5. **Quantified replay + an honest gap** — section 3 compresses the core pattern
   into one data table (extremes / percentile / what followed), closed by a recap;
   surface the caliber trap or lead-lag you found (the honest gap); then corroborate
   with real-world cases, every external number carrying its source.
   **量化复盘 + 诚实 gap**——第 3 节把核心规律压成一张数据表（极值／分位／后续），表后
   收束；主动标出你发现的口径陷阱或领先滞后（诚实 gap）；再用真实世界案例佐证，每个外部
   数字带出处。
   - e.g. *"IC 峰往往领先价格峰——2021 年 IC 在 7 月冲顶，价格涨到 12 月才见顶；所以
     表里的'回撤'是从信号亮起算起、窗口内的最大回撤。"* · 诚实说明领先关系，不掩盖。

6. **Insight stacked in layers** — section 4 climbs: baseline → mechanism/why →
   limit (can / cannot); each layer is one insight, run as four beats, closed on a
   bolded line, with its objection anticipated.
   **层层递进的洞见**——第 4 节往上垒：基线 → 机制／为什么 → 边界（能／不能）；每层一个
   洞见、四拍展开、一句加粗收束、并预判反对。

7. **A causal chain for every surprise** — give the causal chain in plain steps
   (A → B → C); answer "why X and not Y" head-on. A number or verdict with no
   visible "why" reads as an assertion.
   **每个意外都给因果链**——用大白话给出因果链（A → B → C）；正面回答"为什么是 X 不是
   Y"。没有可见"为什么"的数字或结论，读起来是断言。
   - e.g. *"要让中长期排序重新可预测，只有资金把相对格局焊死一种情形 → 这正是抱团 → 所以
     IC 暴涨反推出抱团。"*

8. **Aphoristic close, ringing back** — section 5 closes on one sharp contrastive
   aphorism that rings back to the opening (or the prior piece), not a flat summary;
   then run the self-check below before shipping.
   **格言收尾、首尾呼应**——第 5 节以一句锋利的对照式格言收尾、呼应开篇（或前作），而非
   平铺总结；发布前再跑一遍下面的自检。
   - e.g. *"同一个 IC，一个用来进攻，一个用来避险。"*
   - Anti-pattern (a flat recap): *"综上，本文讲了五点。"*
     反模式（平铺总结）。

## Self-check | 自检清单

- [ ] Does the first screen convey 80% in 30 seconds, and is the title a falsifiable
      finding (not a label)? | 第一屏 30 秒传达八成、标题是可证伪的结论（非标签）？
- [ ] Do the sections form a chain (each answers the previous one's question), not a
      pile? | 各节成链（每节回答上节问题），而非散堆？
- [ ] Does every figure run the four beats (how to read → what it shows → link →
      takeaway)? | 每张图是否走四拍（怎么看 → 看出什么 → 联系 → 收束）？
- [ ] Does every surprising claim show its mechanism (A → B → C), not just a verdict?
      | 每个意外结论是否给了机制链，而非只甩结论？
- [ ] Is every external number sourced in plain words, every derived number shown with
      its arithmetic? | 每个外部数字用人话标了出处、每个推导数字摊开了算式？
- [ ] When two calibers coexist, are they distinguished once and for all (no
      "对不上" confusion)? | 两种口径并存时是否一次讲清（无"对不上"困惑）？
- [ ] Is each persuasive claim met with its own rebuttal-and-answer, and the limit
      stated honestly? | 每个说服性结论是否自带反驳与回答、并诚实标了边界？
- [ ] Is each table followed by a one-line recap, each term unpacked on first use? |
      每张表后是否一句白话收束、每个术语首次出现是否拆开？
- [ ] Are headings short, progressively numbered, free of engineering codes (no
      Fig-XXX in headings)? | 标题是否简短、连续编号、不带工程口径（标题里无 Fig-XXX）？
- [ ] Does the piece close on one carried-away aphorism that rings back, with no
      emoji — and a 非投资建议 note when the subject is a market? |
      全文是否以一句呼应开篇的格言收尾、无 emoji——涉市场时是否标*非投资建议*？
